The three disciplines

What are SEO, AEO
and GEO?

What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the work of making a website rank in Google and Bing results: technical health so crawlers can read every page, content that matches what customers search for, and authority earned through links and mentions. It is the foundation the other two disciplines are built on.

What is AEO?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring content so search engines can lift it as a direct answer — the featured snippet above the results, People Also Ask boxes, and what voice assistants read aloud. The craft is answer-first writing: complete, self-contained answers placed exactly where engines look for them.

What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of making a brand visible and citable inside AI-generated answers — ChatGPT Search, Perplexity, Gemini and Google AI Overviews. AI engines quote pages that are indexable, factually dense and clearly attributable to a consistent entity; GEO engineers all three.

What is the difference between SEO, AEO and GEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) makes a website rank in Google's blue-link results. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) structures content so it is lifted as the direct answer — featured snippets, People Also Ask and voice replies. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) makes a brand visible and citable inside AI answers from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini and Google AI Overviews. They build on each other, which is why TheVincis delivers all three together.

Is GEO worth it for a small business?

Increasingly, yes. A growing share of buyers now ask ChatGPT, Perplexity or Google's AI Overviews for recommendations instead of scrolling results. GEO is mostly disciplined SEO — indexable pages, clear answers, factual content, consistent brand identity — so a small business that invests in it early gets cited while competitors are still invisible to AI engines.

How long does SEO take to show results?

For a new or neglected site: indexing within days to weeks, first long-tail rankings in months 2–3, and top-10 positions on winnable terms in months 3–6. Competitive head terms take 6–12 months of compounding content and authority. Anyone promising faster guaranteed results is selling something.

Do you guarantee a #1 ranking on Google?

No — and no honest agency can. Rankings depend on competition, content quality and authority earned over time. What TheVincis guarantees is the work: measurable technical health, answer-ready content, valid structured data, and a monthly report showing exactly where you rank and whether AI engines cite you.

The standard

What does a small-business website
actually need?

A small-business website earns its keep when it does five jobs: load fast on cheap phones, make the next step obvious, get found by search and AI engines, stay secure, and remain yours to change. Every TheVincis build ships with all five:

  • Speed on real devices — optimized images, minimal scripts and green Core Web Vitals, tested on budget Android phones, not just office laptops.
  • A purpose, not a poster — one clear conversion path per page: call, WhatsApp, booking, order or enquiry. Design decisions answer to that path.
  • SEO · AEO · GEO built in — semantic markup, structured data, answer-first content structure, sitemap and AI-crawler policy from day one, not bolted on later.
  • Security by default — HTTPS, hardened forms, dependency hygiene and DevSecOps discipline carried over from our security background.
  • Full ownership — domain, hosting, code and documentation handed over in your name. No lock-in, no ransom.

Which one do you need?

Business website vs
full-stack platform

A business website presents and converts; a web platform operates — logins, dashboards, payments, workflows. Most businesses start with the first and grow into the second. TheVincis builds both on the same foundation, so the upgrade path is an extension, not a rebuild:

Before you ask

Web development questions,
answered straight.

How much does a small business website cost in India?

It depends on what the website has to do — a lead-generating business site and a full web platform with logins and payments are different projects. TheVincis scopes every project in a free consultation and gives a fixed quote before any work begins, so there are no surprises mid-build.

How long does it take to build?

A purpose-driven business website typically ships in 2–4 weeks from the first call; full-stack platforms with custom features take longer and are scheduled milestone by milestone. You see working previews throughout — never a big reveal at the end.

Will my website rank on Google?

Every TheVincis website ships with technical SEO, AEO answer structure and GEO (AI-search) readiness built in: clean semantic markup, structured data, fast Core Web Vitals, a sitemap and crawler policy. Rankings then depend on content and competition — we offer SEO·AEO·GEO as an ongoing service for that.

Can you redesign or fix my existing website?

Yes. TheVincis audits your current site first — speed, security, SEO and conversion flow — and tells you honestly whether it needs a redesign or a repair. Sometimes a focused fix beats a rebuild; you get that recommendation in writing either way.

Who owns the website and its code?

You do — completely. Domain, hosting, code and content are registered and handed over in your name, with documentation. No lock-in: any developer can maintain what TheVincis builds, though most clients stay for the support.

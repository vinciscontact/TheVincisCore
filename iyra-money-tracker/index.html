How it works

How does automatic UPI
expense tracking work?

Every UPI payment already announces itself — as a notification on your phone. Iyra listens for exactly those alerts and turns them into a complete expense log, so tracking happens whether you remember to or not:

  1. Pay as usual — Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, your bank's app. Nothing about how you pay changes.
  2. Iyra catches the notification — the amount and payee are captured the moment the payment alert appears.
  3. Logged and categorised — the expense lands in your ledger; confirm or change the category in one tap.
  4. The household sees one truth — every family member's transactions in one shared view, tagged by person.
  5. Your money explains itself — budgets, savings rate, cashflow trends and category insights, updated live.

Before you ask

Iyra questions,
answered straight.

How does Iyra track UPI transactions automatically?

When you pay with any UPI app, your phone shows a payment notification. Iyra reads that notification, captures the amount and payee, and logs the expense for you — no typing, no forgotten transactions. You can review, recategorise or edit any entry afterwards.

Which UPI apps does Iyra work with?

Iyra works with any UPI app that shows payment notifications — Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, bank apps and others. Because it reads the notification rather than the app itself, new UPI apps work without waiting for an update.

Can my family use Iyra together?

Yes. Iyra is built around the household: one shared view of the family's money, with each transaction tagged by member. You see who spent what, combined budgets and one savings rate — instead of four separate apps that never agree.

Is my financial data safe with Iyra?

Iyra uses notification access only to detect payment alerts and log the amount and payee — it never asks for your bank login, UPI PIN or OTPs, and it cannot move money. Built by TheVincis, a studio with a security-first engineering background.

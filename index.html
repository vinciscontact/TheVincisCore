What does TheVincis do?

TheVincis is a digital product studio that builds SaaS products, full-stack web platforms, custom mobile applications, purpose-driven portfolio websites and data analytics systems. It also provides SEO, AEO and GEO services. The studio focuses on tier-2 and tier-3 businesses that need enterprise-grade technology at accessible scale.

What is TableServe by TheVincis?

TableServe is TheVincis's flagship QR ordering system for restaurants and cafés. Customers scan a QR code at their table, browse a live digital menu on their phone, and place orders that reach the kitchen instantly — no app download, no printed menus, fewer order errors and faster table turnover.

Who does TheVincis work with?

TheVincis works primarily with tier-2 and tier-3 businesses — growing companies in smaller cities that face real operational problems but are usually priced out of enterprise-grade software. Every website and system is built to solve a specific business problem, not just to look good.

What is the difference between SEO, AEO and GEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps a website rank in Google search results. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) structures content so it is picked for featured snippets, People-Also-Ask boxes and voice assistants. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) makes a brand visible and citable inside AI answers from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini and Google AI Overviews. TheVincis implements all three together.
